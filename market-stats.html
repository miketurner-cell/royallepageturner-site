Market Insights

Area-by-Area Performance

2025 annual summaries and 2026 YTD highlights for each service area.

Gander (A1V)

2025 Annual Performance

  • Strong price appreciation: $338K average (2025 YTD)
  • Market activity stable with 79-day average DOM
  • Inventory balance supporting buyer and seller confidence

2026 YTD Highlights

  • $381K average sale price to date
  • 38% year-over-year price growth, signaling strong demand
  • Central Newfoundland's largest and most active market

Market Insight: Gander continues to lead the province in pricing strength and transaction velocity. First-time buyers and relocating families are driving consistent demand.

Happy Valley–Goose Bay

2025 Annual Performance

  • Market correction from 2024 peaks; $326K average
  • Quick turnover: 56-day average DOM indicates healthy activity
  • Labrador's largest population center with government-led stability

2026 YTD Highlights

  • $287K average sale price YTD
  • 20% year-over-year decline from 2025 peaks—price normalization
  • Government employment sector remains primary driver

Market Insight: Price correction is creating renewed buyer interest in this Labrador hub. Stable employment and community amenities continue to support transaction volume.

Labrador West

2025 Annual Performance

  • Steady appreciation: $337K average (2025 YTD)
  • Quick sales turnover: 54-day average DOM
  • Mining sector activity supporting broader economic confidence

2026 YTD Highlights

  • $387K average sale price to date
  • 15% year-over-year growth; strongest Labrador performer
  • Lab City and Wabush both showing upward momentum

Market Insight: Labrador West's resource sector foundations are supporting price stability and buyer confidence. Growing interest from out-of-province relocations.

St. John's Metro

2025 Annual Performance

  • Highest price tier: $380K average across capital region
  • Fastest turnover: 33-day average DOM province-wide
  • 18 distinct community submarkets with varying dynamics

2026 YTD Highlights

  • $409K average sale price to date
  • Robust demand from corporate relocations and local buyers
  • Metropolitan amenities driving premium valuations

Market Insight: St. John's metro remains Canada's fastest-selling market in this price range. Urban amenities and economic diversity create sustained buyer demand.
