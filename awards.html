Highest Brokerage Honour

A.E. LePage Award: Brokerage of the Year — East Coast

Awarded 2018 & 2020

Named after Royal LePage's founder, the A.E. LePage Award is the highest honour the company bestows on a brokerage. It recognizes ethical, professional operation and a deep commitment to the community.

Royal LePage Turner Realty was named Brokerage of the Year for the East Coast region in both 2018 and 2020 — a distinction shared by very few brokerages in Atlantic Canada.

Technology Leadership

Royal LePage Best in Tech Award

Awarded 2021, 2022 & 2024

Recognizes brokerages that lead in technology adoption for marketing and client service — including professional photography, cinematic video tours, iGuide 3D walkthroughs, and targeted digital marketing campaigns.

Turner Realty has earned this award three times, reflecting our investment in giving every listing the presentation it deserves and ensuring clients have access to the best tools available.

Smart Leads Leadership

Royal LePage Lead Manager of the Year — National Finalist

2023 · 3rd Nationally

Mike Turner was named a national finalist for Royal LePage Lead Manager of the Year in 2023, placing 3rd in the country with a 5.4% Smart Leads close rate. This data-driven award measures how effectively a brokerage converts online leads into closed transactions.

That same year, Mike was selected as one of three national panelists for the Royal LePage Smart Leads broker webinar, presenting alongside top-performing brokerages from British Columbia and Newfoundland on lead generation strategy.

"Augment Your Pipeline" — National Smart Leads Webinar, March 2023
Community Service

Royal LePage Shelter Foundation Awards — East Coast

Team Award 2018 · Brokerage Award 2024

Karen Pomeroy and Roberta Primmer of our Happy Valley–Goose Bay office won the Shelter Foundation Team Award for the East Coast in 2018, recognized for launching an annual Masquerade Ball fundraiser for Libra House. The event has been held four times, raising over $35,000 for domestic violence services in Labrador.

In 2024, the brokerage itself earned the Shelter Foundation Brokerage Award for the East Coast — peer-nominated recognition for our ongoing commitment across all offices, including our annual 12 Business Days of Christmas donation drive supporting both Libra House in Happy Valley–Goose Bay and Cara Transition House in Gander.
