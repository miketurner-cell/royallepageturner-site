Sources & Methodology

  • Data source: Loft47 Production Report, filtered by CloseDate.
  • Volume = listing-side amount + selling-side amount per deal (ends basis).
  • Units = transaction ends (each side counted as 1; 0.5 for co-listing/co-selling).
  • Award Year = Nov 16 – Nov 15, matching Royal LePage awards program.
  • Pending = firm + conditional deals not yet closed (Loft47 "unpaid" view). Shown per agent as units / commission $. Pending units are added to projected year-end and Pace Tier calculations.
  • Active Listings = current inventory on MLS (not yet under contract). Sourced from NLAR Matrix saved search "Turner Realty - Active Listings (All Offices)" — pinned as a Favourite on the Matrix home tab. Refresh cadence: every 2 weeks. Co-listed deals count as 0.5 per agent (matches Loft47 split-credit convention), so per-agent values reconcile to the brokerage unique-listing total (currently 35) without double-counting.
  • Goose Bay deals are typically co-listed by Karen Pomeroy and Roberta Primmer at 50/50 — brokerage totals reflect the full deal, agent rows reflect each agent's share.
  • Award tiers use the 2025 Royal LePage East Coast Provincial Fixed Value thresholds, plus the two national tiers (Chairman's Club = Top 1%, National Top 10 = top 10 earners). Official qualification is based on GCI or Units (whichever is met); this dashboard overlays Units only, because GCI is not captured in the Loft47 source data used here. Individual (1–1.25) thresholds apply to all Gander agents; Small Team (1.26–3.99) thresholds apply to Karen + Roberta (combined team units).
  • Chairman's Club 2025 thresholds are estimates (>$1.05M GCI or >75 units) — finalized Dec 12, 2025. National Top 10 entry levels vary year-to-year; the dashboard uses 2024 historical as a proxy.
  • Pace Tier projects an agent's full-year 2026 units at the current daily rate (plus pending) and shows the corresponding 2025 award tier they would qualify for.
  • Gap line under Pace Tier shows exactly what it takes to clear the next tier up — the single most actionable line for 1:1 coaching.
  • 2026 Goal = per-agent personal goal in units (currently set at ~+10% over AY 2025; editable in the DATA const). Dark bar = closed YTD, striped bar = pending.
  • Coaching Notes save to this browser only (localStorage). They persist between updates on the same device — if you use a different browser/device, you'll see a fresh set.
  • Print with ⌘-P / Ctrl-P for a 2-up agent-card handout suitable for 1:1s (hero, history, and charts are hidden in print).
